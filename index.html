my country and my city

Dublin, Ireland’s vibrant capital, is a city where history meets modern culture. With its cobbled streets, Georgian architecture, and literary legacy, Dublin offers a unique blend of charm and creativity. From the historic Trinity College to the lively Temple Bar, there’s always something to explore. my favorite book

Diary Of A Wimpy Kid

*Diary of a Wimpy Kid* is similar to a series of episodes, and you can find the books in most libraries. The story follows the everyday life of a family, with main characters including Georgie, his brothers Rodrick and Manny, and his best friend Rowley. Manny, being the youngest, is the favorite of the family. The book centers on Georgie, a middle schooler, who, along with Rowley, gets into all sorts of crazy adventures and trouble. For example, Georgie and Rowley once accidentally put a hole in the ceiling of Georgie's house.
